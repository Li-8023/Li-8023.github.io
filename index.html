About Me

I am a graduate student at Georgia Tech, having graduated summa cum laude from Arizona State University. I excel in web development and full-stack development. I have completed two internships at China Telecom, a global leader in telecommunications. My first internship was as a Software Development Engineer, and the second was as a Frontend Developer. In these roles, I specialized in both frontend and backend development, crafting comprehensive web applications that enhance user experience and operational efficiency.

My research interests lie in the field of human-computer interaction. Outside of my professional pursuits, I am passionate about Latin dance, an activity that allows me to express creativity and maintain physical fitness. I also enjoy skiing, which not only challenges me but also provides a thrilling way to experience the great outdoors.
